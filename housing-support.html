Housing & Support Services

Providing a stable, supportive, and faith-based living environment.

Our Housing Model

We acquire and manage multi-unit residential properties (typically 4-8 units) that have been carefully vetted to comply with California's complex PC 290 residency laws[cite: 24, 48]. This ensures a safe and legally compliant living space for our participants. We provide a stable, supportive, faith-based living environment[cite: 39].

Our housing provides a foundation for personal growth, allowing participants to focus on their training and employment with peace of mind.

Holistic Support Services

To address all aspects of reentry, our program offers a range of optional support services to help participants become productive, integrated members of society. These services include:

  • Faith-based counseling and spiritual support [cite: 44]
  • Life skills workshops and financial literacy training [cite: 69]
  • Mentoring and one-on-one guidance [cite: 69]
  • Connections to external community resources as needed [cite: 70]

These services are an integral part of our mission to foster spiritual, personal, and professional transformation[cite: 15].

Our Goal

Our goal is to achieve low recidivism rates among program participants by providing an integrated solution that addresses the unique challenges they face[cite: 32]. By offering compliant housing, vocational training, guaranteed employment, and holistic support, we offer a comprehensive solution to the barriers to successful reentry[cite: 37].

Learn More About Our Integrated Approach

The combination of our services is what makes our program a leading model for effective reentry[cite: 20]. For more details on our vocational training and employment opportunities, please visit our Vocational Training & Employment page.
